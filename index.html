Projects

Tableau Project I: Titanic

In this project, I analyzed data from the sinking of the Titanic to check whether survival rate had anything to do with the passenger class. Also, I assessed the age of the passengers within each class to identify vulnerable age categories. The analysis and dashboard reveal significant disparities in survival rates among Titanic passengers based on class and age, which is crucial for designing safer ships.

Featured Publication

This report evaluates two support projects in Transfrontier Conservation Areas (TFCAs) in Southern Africa, specifically in the Malawi-Zambia TFCA and the Lubombo TFCA. The evaluation uses OECD-DAC criteria to assess the relevance, effectiveness, efficiency, impact, and sustainability of these projects, which aimed to harmonize nature conservation with the socio-economic development of local communities. **Findings highlight successes in areas like cross-border governance, community engagement, and alternative livelihoods, while also pointing out challenges related to sustainability and stakeholder coordination. The report offers recommendations for implementing partners, communities, national governments, and donors to improve the design and implementation of future TFCA projects. Ultimately, the study underscores the importance of integrating community needs and participation for effective and long-lasting conservation outcomes in these vital ecological regions.
